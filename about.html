About Me

Hello, I'm Jonathan Alvarado. I have been living in the beautiful island of Siquijor in the Philippines. I have always had a passion for creativity, especially in the realm of video and photo editing. Since high school, I have been honing my skills in editing videos, photos, and even music.

In pursuit of my passion, I enrolled in a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program and successfully completed my first year. However, my journey doesn't stop there. I am currently focusing on expanding my knowledge and skills in web development.

With a deep interest in computers and a commitment to continuous learning, I am dedicated to exploring the ever-evolving world of video and photo editing. Additionally, I am currently expanding my skills in web development, studying the art of crafting engaging and interactive websites. I strive to combine my expertise in video and photo editing with my growing knowledge of web development to create visually stunning and user-friendly online experiences. I am excited to apply my creativity and technical skills to both realms, bringing captivating visuals and seamless functionality to the digital world.

Aside from my professional pursuits, I also enjoy playing online games as a pastime activity. In fact, I have a Facebook gaming page called Jo Ensal. I invite you to check it out and join me on my gaming adventures.

If you have any further questions or would like to know more about my work and experiences, feel free to reach out. I am always eager to connect and collaborate.
